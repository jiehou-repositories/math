Vector Spaces

Definition of vector spaces

  1. A set \(V\).
  2. Functions:
    • Addition \(+:\ V \times V \rightarrow V\)
    • Scalar multiplication \(\cdot:\ \mathbb{C} \times V \rightarrow V\)
  3. Conditions:
    • Commutativity: \(\forall\ \textbf{u}, \textbf{v} \in V,\ +(\textbf{u}, \textbf{v}) = +(\textbf{v}, \textbf{u})\).
    • Associativity: \(\forall\ \textbf{u}, \textbf{v}, \textbf{w} \in V\) and \(\forall\ \lambda, \mu \in \mathbb{C}\), \(+(+(\textbf{u}, \textbf{v}), \textbf{w}) = +(\textbf{u}, +(\textbf{v}, \textbf{w}))\) and \(\cdot(\cdot(\lambda, \mu), \textbf{v}) = \cdot(\lambda, \cdot(\mu, \textbf{v}))\).
    • Additive identity: \(\exists\ \textbf{0} \in V\), \(\forall\ \textbf{v} \in V\), \(+(\textbf{v}, \textbf{0}) = \textbf{v}\).
    • Additive inverse: \(\forall\ \textbf{v} \in V\), \(\exists\ \textbf{u} \in V\), \(+(\textbf{v}, \textbf{u}) = \textbf{0}\).
    • Multiplicative identity: \(\forall\ \textbf{v} \in V,\ \cdot(1, \textbf{v}) = \textbf{v}\).
    • Distributive properties: \(\forall\ \textbf{u}, \textbf{v} \in V\) and \(\forall\ \lambda, \mu \in \mathbb{C}\), \(\cdot(\lambda, +(\textbf{u}, \textbf{v})) = +(\cdot(\lambda, \textbf{u}), \cdot(\lambda, \textbf{v}))\) and \(\cdot(+(\lambda, \mu), \textbf{v}) = +(\cdot(\lambda, \textbf{v}), \cdot(\mu, \textbf{v}))\).

Common vector spaces

\(\mathbb{R}\)

  1. A set \(\mathbb{R}\).
  2. Functions:
    • Addition \(+:\ (\mathbb{R}, \mathbb{R}) \rightarrow \mathbb{R}\)
      \(\forall\ \lambda, \mu \in \mathbb{R}, +(\lambda, \mu) = \lambda + \mu\).
    • Scalar multiplication \(\cdot:\ (\mathbb{C}, V) \rightarrow V\)
      \(\forall\ \lambda, \mu \in \mathbb{R}, \cdot(\lambda, \mu) = \lambda\mu\).
  3. Properties:
    • Commutativity: \(\forall\ u, v \in \mathbb{R},\ u + v = v + u\).
    • Associativity: \(\forall\ u, v, w \in \mathbb{R}\) and \(\forall\ \lambda, \mu \in \mathbb{C}\), \((u + v) + w = u + (v + w)\) and \((\lambda\mu) u = \lambda(\mu u)\).
    • Additive identity: \(\exists\ 0 \in \mathbb{R}\), \(\forall\ \lambda \in \mathbb{R}\), \(\lambda + 0 = \lambda\).
    • Additive inverse: \(\forall\ v \in \mathbb{R}\), \(\exists\ u \in \mathbb{R}\), \(v + u = 0\).
    • Multiplicative identity: \(\forall\ v \in \mathbb{R},\ 1 \cdot v = v\).
    • Distributive properties: \(\forall\ u, v \in \mathbb{R}\) and \(\forall\ \lambda, \mu \in \mathbb{C}\), \((\lambda + \mu) v = \lambda v + \mu v\) and \(\lambda(u + v) = \lambda u + \lambda v\).
